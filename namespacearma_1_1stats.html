Generate Gaussian kernel with radius \(r\) and standard deviation \(\sigma\).

Parameters
rkernel radius
sigmastandard deviation
Date
2018-02-02
Author
Ivan Gankevich
  • The size of the kernel equals \(2r+1\).
  • The kernel formula is \(\exp\left[-\frac{x^2}{2\sigma}\right]\).
  • The sum of all points equals 1.
