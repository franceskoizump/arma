Expands ACF into Gram—Charlier series.

Date
2017-05-20
Author
Ivan Gankevich
Parameters
[in]apolynomial coefficients of a transformed cumulative distribution function
[in]max_ordermaximal expansion order
[in]acf_varianceACF variance \(\gamma_{\vec{0}}\)
[out]errerror of the resulting approximation
See also
transform_CDF
  1. Calculate series coefficients \(C\):

    \[ C_i = p_0 + \begin{cases} \sum\limits_{n=2}^{m} p_n (n-1)!! & \text{if }n\text{ is even},\\ 0 & \text{if }n\text{ is odd}, \end{cases} \]

    where \(m\) is the current polynomial order and \(p_n\) is a coefficient before \(n\)-th order polynomial term.
  2. Calculate error:

    \[ e_m = \left| \sum\limits_{n=0}^{m} \frac{C_i^2}{(n+1)!} - \gamma_{\vec{0}} \right|. \]
