Detailed Description

template<int p = 521, int w = 32>
struct arma::prng::parallel_mt_seq< p, w >

Generates a sequence of mt_config objects.

mt_config objects are used to initialise parallel_mt generators to make them produce uncorrelated sequences of pseudo-random numbers in parallel.
