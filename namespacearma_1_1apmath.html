Computes Owen's \(T\)-function.

Date
2017-06-04
Author
Ivan Gankevich

Uses Gauss integration formula from patefield2000fast

\[ T(h, \alpha) = \frac{\alpha}{2\pi} \sum\limits_{i=1}^{m} \omega_i \exp\left[ -\frac{1}{2}h^2 \left( 1 + \alpha^2x_i^2 \right) \right] \frac{1}{1 + \alpha^2x_i^2} \]

with weights \(\omega_i\) computed via Legendre polynomials abramowitz1972handbook (eq. 25.4.40):

\[ \omega_i = \frac{2}{\left(1 - x_i^2 \right) P_n'(x_i)^2}, \]

where \(P_n\) is Legendre polynomial of order \(n\). Abscissas \(x_i\) are the roots of this polynomial. Uses 10th order polynomial.
