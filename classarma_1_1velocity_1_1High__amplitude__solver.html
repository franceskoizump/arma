Compute multiplier function as

\[ F(x, y) = \frac{ \zeta_t }{ (\zeta_x + \zeta_y) / \sqrt{\smash[b]{1 + \zeta_x^2 + \zeta_y^2}} - \zeta_x - \zeta_y - 1 } \]

instead of \(F(x,y)=\zeta_t\) as in linear case.
